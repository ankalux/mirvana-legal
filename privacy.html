Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

At Mirvana, we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • User Images: Only used temporarily for AI processing. We do not store or retain them after generation is complete.
  • Anonymous User ID: Created via Firebase for usage tracking purposes.
  • Usage Metrics: Used to monitor image generation limits and app performance.

Subscriptions

Mirvana is available via a monthly subscription. Payments are handled securely through Apple. We do not access or store your payment information.

Third Parties

We use Firebase for anonymous login, Firestore for usage tracking, and Replicate for AI image generation. These services have their own privacy policies and comply with GDPR and other regulations.

GDPR Rights

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any data we may have collected. Contact us to make such a request.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy or data usage, please email us at privacy@ankalux.org.
